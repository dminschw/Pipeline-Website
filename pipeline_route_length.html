Route length is computed as great-circle distance between the points you place, summed across all segments — it does not account for terrain, elevation change, or routing around obstacles, so treat it as a preliminary straight-line-corridor estimate. Copy the resulting mileage into the "Pipe length (miles)" field on the sizing workbench.
