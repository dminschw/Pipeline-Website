Route length is computed as great-circle distance between the points you place or import, summed across all segments — it does not account for terrain, elevation change, or routing around obstacles, so treat it as a preliminary straight-line-corridor estimate. Imported files are parsed entirely in your browser; nothing is uploaded to a server. For shapefiles, upload a .zip containing the .shp/.dbf/.prj/.shx files together so the coordinates are reprojected correctly — a lone .shp file is read as-is and assumed to already be in WGS84 lat/lon. Copy the resulting mileage into the "Pipe length (miles)" field on the sizing workbench.
